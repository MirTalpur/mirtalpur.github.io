About Piing!

Piing is a product that allows seemless transaction between a business and a consumer. Both the business and individual create a profile that includes the services they provide alongside their price. Individuals have the opportunity to create an independent job market for themselves. They will also have the opportunity to post on the job board specifying the details of what is requested as well as their price range. Business profiles are set up in a manner to allow users to search and view products offered by a company and compare pricing and customer satisfaction ratings, also allowing the consumer to pay for a product in one convenient location. Allowing this ease of access between business and consumer, Piing can create countless opportunities.
